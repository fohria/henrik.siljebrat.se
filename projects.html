Planet Ghibli

I always wanted to see BBC's Planet Earth in the style of a Studio Ghibli film. This is the result of a few days of play with neural networks and style transfer. I also use the process to discuss how machine learning involves trial and error, pointing out the importance of thinking about the data you use, and potential societal impact of black box systems.
